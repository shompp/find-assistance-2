About Us

Find Assistance, founded in 2022, was established with a mission to build a sophisticated and responsive claims department for the insurance market.

Our commitment to nurturing quality individuals, empowering them to perform at their highest capabilities, and offering them a stepping stone into the insurance market distinguishes us from our competitors.

  • Specializing in a specific niche has enabled us to understand and cater to the unique needs of our clients.
  • Our team comprises of industry experts who listen, comprehend core values, and take action based on our clients' specific needs.
  • Despite our short period of operation, we have already incorporated three clients and have a growing team of over 25 dedicated individuals providing their services to the insurance market.

At Find Assistance, we view our success through the lens of our clients' success. It's why we tirelessly work towards providing exceptional services that not only meet but exceed client expectations. By combining our industry expertise with a deep understanding of our clients' needs, we're creating powerful, lasting partnerships within the insurance market. Discover how our dedicated team can provide you with the tools, resources, and guidance to propel your business forward.

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand you might have questions about how Find Assistance can support your business. To make things easier, we've compiled a list of frequently asked questions about our services and processes. If you don't find the answer you're looking for, don't hesitate to get in touch. We're here to assist you!

  • What does "Find Assistance" do exactly?

    Find Assistance is a company specializing in providing support to the insurance market. Our services include human resources management, consulting, payroll processes, risk reduction in hiring, comprehensive training, and cost-effective solutions. We cater to both online and in-person needs, with the latter currently available only in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

  • How does "Find Assistance" help reduce costs and risks in hiring?

    At Find Assistance, we have a team of HR specialists who streamline the recruitment and hiring process to identify the best talent suited for your needs. We carry out thorough background checks and ensure legal compliance, mitigating any potential hiring risks. Furthermore, our training program is designed to reduce the learning curve, thereby saving time and costs related to onboarding and training new staff.

  • How can I avail of "Find Assistance" services?

    It's quite simple. You can reach out to us via email or phone call. We are available from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. You can also schedule a meeting or call at your convenience. We're ready to help you streamline your processes and boost your business efficiency.
